Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

At QR Lite, your privacy is our highest priority. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. 100% Offline Scanning

QR Lite is built as an offline-first utility. We do not upload your scan results, images, or history to any servers. All data stays strictly on your device.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). There is no sign-up, no email tracking, and no profile creation required to use the app.

3. Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to display banner ads. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can reset this ID in your Android settings.

4. Permissions

We only ask for Camera Access. This is used exclusively for scanning codes. We do not record video or save photos without your explicit action.
